Delete Your Account

We respect your right to delete your account at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the Jitak mobile app:

  1. Open the Jitak app.
  2. Go to the Side Menu.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account.

Need Help?

If you experience any issues while deleting your account, please contact our support team at:
jitakteam@gmail.com

Once deleted, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
